Roopkund Trek

  • Viyomi Shah
  • 12 june, 2019
  • 10

A few months ago Sonali Bhagchandani and I were contemplating about our plans for the summer, when we came across the Roopkund Trek by The Adventurers Himalayan. Little did we know what we were signing up for..what it would be like to walk for hours everyday or to live in tents. To be able to witness the sight of several hundred human skeletons at the renowned 'Skeletal Lake' was an experience we had never dreamt of. The trek was undoubtedly one of a kind and through which we met some amazing, inspiring people. Truly," You don't need to be a hero to do certain things-to compete. You can just be an ordinary chap, sufficiently motivated to reach challenging goals." - Edmund Hillary
